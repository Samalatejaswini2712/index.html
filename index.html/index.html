Wild life

1. Wildlife refers to all living organisms that are not domesticated and exist in their natural habitats.

2. Wildlife includes a wide variety of species, including mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, insects.

3. Wildlife plays a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance and biodiversity.

4. Conservation efforts are essential to protect wildlife and their habitats from threats such as habitat loss, poaching and climate change.

5. Many organisations and individuals work towards wildlife conservation through education, awareness, and sustainable practices.
